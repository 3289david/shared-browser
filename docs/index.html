Everything you need to browse together

Built for the way people actually use the web with others.

Live Cursors

See everyone's cursor in real-time, labeled with their name. Like Google Docs, but for the entire web.

Annotations

Highlight text, drop sticky notes, or draw on any page. All annotations are visible to everyone in real-time.

Built-in Chat

Chat sidebar appears on every page. No need to switch between apps while browsing together.

Shared History

Every page visited during the session is tracked with a timeline. See who went where and when.

Split & Merge

Break off to explore independently, then merge back with one click. No one has to wait.

Event Replay

Record your session as events, not video. Tiny file sizes. Scrub through any moment of your browsing session.

Four ways to browse together

Switch modes anytime. The session adapts to what you need right now.

01

Follow Mode

All participants follow the leader's navigation. When the leader moves to a new page, everyone follows automatically. Perfect for walkthroughs, tutorials, and guided research.

Education / Demos
02

Free Mode

Everyone browses independently. You can see where others are and jump to their page anytime with one click. Great for research where you want to cover more ground.

Research / Exploration
03

Group Mode

Any navigation by any member moves everyone. Collaborative and equal - no single leader. Best for shopping or trip planning where everyone contributes.

Shopping / Planning
04

Presentation Mode

Audience follows the presenter only. Audience cannot change the shared view, but can still react, chat, and see each other's cursors on the page.

Presentations / Teaching

Built for real situations

Shopping Together

Browse, compare, vote on items, and share carts - all in real-time. No more screenshotting products into group chats.

Travel Planning

Research hotels, flights, and restaurants together. Annotate pages with "this one!" and build a shared itinerary in real-time.

Remote Support

Help family or colleagues navigate a website. See their cursor, guide them step by step, or take control when needed.

Study Sessions

Read articles, highlight passages, and discuss in real-time. The teacher can lead the session or let students explore freely.

How to install

1
Download the .zip for your browser and extract it
2
Open your browser's extensions page and enable Developer Mode
3
Click "Load unpacked" and select the extracted folder
4
Click the extension icon, enter your name, and start a session
